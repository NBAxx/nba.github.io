About Our Team

Oasis Builders is a full-service remodeling company that specializes in turning dreams into a reality through remodeling and home renovations. Here at Oasis Builders, we specialize in bathrooms, kitchens, additions, ADU’s and general remodeling throughout the house. With a unique ability to combine field expertise, professional management and outstanding customer service, Oasis Builders is your true partner for a smooth and affordable home remodeling project.

/ Who We Are

Our company is family-owned and operated, and we service clients throughout the Greater Los Angeles area. We are licensed, bonded, and insured, and most importantly, we are a trusted name in the business with a solid reputation for excellence, reliability, and high-quality work. Our experienced and dedicated team is here to accompany you every step of the way– from design, all the way to a turnkey project. Our priority is to ensure your satisfaction and peace of mind, and our goal is to provide you with superb results.
